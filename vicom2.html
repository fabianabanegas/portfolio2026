Born on the Moon

Science Fiction Film Poster

Solo project Visual Communication coursework Concept and visual creation

objective

Objective

Communicate hope, curiosity, and gratitude toward Earth through the story of the first human born on the Moon. Original Salar de Uyuni photography captures the protagonist's journey rediscovering human roots.

research

Research

Analyzed emotional sci-fi posters: Dune, Interstellar, Life of Pi. Epic journeys and vast landscapes create isolation and wonder.

Key findings:

  • Typography: Bold geometric sans-serif, all-caps titles
  • Scale: Small figures vs. vast landscapes / negative space
  • Color: Cool skies + warm earth tones for mystery / hope
  • Layout: Centered title + bottom credits block

typography

Typography choices

  • Terano (Primary): Futuristic geometric sans-serif for the title
  • Avignon (Secondary): Clean hierarchy for credits / release info
  • White text maximizes image color impact per classic sci-fi convention

Key learnings
